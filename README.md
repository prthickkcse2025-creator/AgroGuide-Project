# AgroGuide-Project
Link: https://agroguide-prototype-19mi.bolt.host

AgroGuide is a digital platform designed to empower farmers through technology by connecting them with essential agricultural resources, services, and communities
**Farm Share**Facilitates farm equipment sharing between farmers to reduce cost and improve productivity.
  **Rent System**: Displays rental rate (₹/day) and allows booking directly from the app
   **Include Other features such as:**
     > Search & Filter
     > Owner Details
     > Affordable Access
     > Location-based Listing

**Khazana Sabja** Comprises a direct farmer-to-retail connection module eliminating middlemen —helping farmers sell their crops in bulk.
**Nearby Farmers List**: Shows crops and farm names based on proximity.
  **Two Modes:**
 	Showcase: Farmers can list available produce.
 	Register: Retailers can register and contact farmers directly.

   **Include Other features such as:**

     > Distance Indicator(Displays how far the farm is)
     > Direct Booking
     > Transparency( Ensures fair pricing and eliminates intermediaries.)

**Rozana Wages** Connects farm owners with daily wage workers, ensuring steady employment and efficient labour management
 
   **Job Search**: Workers can search for available daily-wage farming jobs.

   **Include Other features such as:**
     > Farm Listings
     > One-click Registration
     > Real-time Updates
     > Fair Wage Access
